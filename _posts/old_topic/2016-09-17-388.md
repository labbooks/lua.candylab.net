查看TCP链接状态

```lua
netstat -n | awk '/^tcp/ {++S[$NF]} END {for(a in S) print a, S[a]}'

ps -A -o stat,ppid,pid,cmd | grep -e '^[Zz]' | awk '{print $2}' |

ps -A -o stat,ppid,pid,cmd | grep -e '^[Zz]' | awk '{print $2}' | xargs kill -9

```


查看假死进程

```lua
 ps -A -o stat,ppid,pid,cmd | grep -e '^[Zz]'
```
